Job summary

Westerburg Academies Trust is a growing multi-academy trust with opportunities across our academies. We seek excellent teachers to join our strong and highly motivated team. Teachers joining our schools benefit from:

  • fantastic ongoing development and support through our designated national Teaching School and Maths Hub
  • a structured induction programme (three paid weeks of induction in July for all NQTs starting in September, plus a further ten full days of training during the first year of teaching)
  • weekly INSET sessions and time with a dedicated in-school mentor
  • many opportunities for progression within each school and across our trust.

This is an exciting time for us and we want the best people to join us as we grow. We offer excellent professional development and career opportunities. To find the best teachers, we keep recruitment open all year. Join us:

To grow:

  • A commitment to recruit, retain and develop excellent staff
  • An entitlement to high quality training and support
  • A supportive working culture, with ongoing guidance and mentoring

To shine:

  • Excellent professional development at all levels
  • Opportunities to work across our trust
  • Opportunities to lead projects and undertake research

To make a difference:

  • An ambitions, fast growing trust with exciting potential
  • A relentless drive for improvement, excellence and equality
  • A focus on excellent pedagogy, positive learning behaviour and high expectations for all

About Westerburg Academies Trust

Westerburg Academies Trust is a high performing chain of primary and secondary academies in England. Our core belief is that children come first, and that every child, irrespective of their background has the right to an outstanding education. We are seeking to employ self-motivated individuals who gain pleasure in the achievements of the children in their class - teachers who have the passion and drive to do whatever it takes for the children they teach to make consistently good progress.
