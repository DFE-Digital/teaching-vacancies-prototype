Job summary

We believe that our children deserve only the best quality staff so recruit carefully and invest in training to ensure continues improvement and an excellent quality of education.

We are looking for an ambitious and experienced middle leader and teacher of science who:

  • has successful experience of leading whole school improvement initiatives specifically in one of the following areas: teaching and learning, behaviour, achievement and personal development and ethos.
  • has successful experience of working collaboratively
  • has a passion for education and a deep-felt desire to make a difference for young people.

The post involves teaching across the year groups and ability ranges at two schools located within one mile of each other. A commitment to excellent pastoral care, enriching co-curricular activities and outreach activities is expected.

About Ridgemont Academies Trust

Ridgemont Academies Trust is a partnership of 42 schools across England. We are committed to our vision: ‘The best for everyone, the best from everyone’. Everything that we do is underpinned by our commitment and values of community, integrity and passion. We never forget that we are here in the service of children, families and communities. In order to get the best from our young people we need to invest in our workforce providing opportunities for people to work collaboratively, to share good practice, and to be supportive, challenging and fair to one another whilst recognising that we are always learning.
