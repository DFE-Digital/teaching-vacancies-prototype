Job summary

Springwood Academies Trust are looking to appoint a dynamic, inspirational, hard-working Primary Teacher to join our academy on a full time basis in September 2020. The role will involve teaching pupils across the full age and ability of Key Stage 1 and 2 in order to ensure the highest possible standards of pupil achievement, personal development and well-being.

Applications for these posts will be considered for any vacancies at the following academies. This will be discussed with you in more detail at the interview and you will be given the opportunity to express your preference(s):

  • Ewen Academy
  • Hills Academy
  • Woodsboro Academy
  • Haddonfield Academy
  • Lakeview Academy

Employee benefits

You’ll find working here rewarding in more ways than one, as our Together Platform offers a comprehensive package of benefits. You will enjoy a health cash plan, shopping discounts, cycle-to-work and gym schemes, cinema tickets and an employee assistance programme – plus all the satisfaction of teaching students who love to learn and grow.

About Springwood Academies Trust

Springwood Academies Trust is right at the forefront of education, both in terms of ensuring students are taught for deep understanding, and in enabling every student to leave school with a qualification that opens doors for them. We have a curriculum that allows for this and the training and support for teachers to feel confident to facilitate this for all.

We have a great training package for NQTs and teachers new to the academy including: Teaching for Mastery; Open Classroom events; TSST; Reasoning at KS3; weekly professional development time and further programmes free through the Springwood Institute of Education. We invest in you, so you invest in our students.

We welcome applications from teachers of any experience, who love teaching and have a passion to develop young minds. You could progress quickly across our Family of Schools if that was what you were looking for, through leadership but, if you just want to be the best teacher you can be, then we are also the Trust for you!
