Job summary

The school is seeking to appoint a dynamic and inspirational teacher to join the senior leadership team to help to realise the vision and ambitions of the school. The specific responsibilities will be agreed with the successful candidate.

This post represents an exciting opportunity to work with the leadership team on our journey to outstanding. The person appointed will play an active part in whole school leadership and management. All members of the leadership team have a range of whole school responsibilities and line management accountabilities for curriculum areas. The Leadership Team also takes on responsibility for maintaining positive behaviour around the school on a day-to-day basis and modelling the high standards and expectations we have of all staff and students.

We believe that this is an ideal post for someone who is ambitious, prepared to work hard and firmly committed to developing the life chances of all young people. This is an excellent professional opportunity and a route to the higher echelons of school leadership. You may be an outstanding middle leader ready to take the next career step or be a more experienced senior leadership member who relishes the exciting challenge of leading.

About Bronson Alcott Academy

Bronson Alcott Academy is a co-educational comprehensive school based in South Yorkshire for students aged 11-16. It is a non-denominational school.

The heart of the school building, a solid stone quadrangle enclosing an impressive dome which houses the library, dates back to 1912 whilst the rest of the school is modern and purpose built, much of it within the last 15 years.

Class sizes are deliberately kept small at the school. Outside the classroom, the school also organises many trips ranging from day trips to art galleries or museums in London, to overseas visits.
